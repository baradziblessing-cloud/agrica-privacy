Agrica Driver – Account Deletion

This page explains how you can request deletion of your Agrica Driver account and personal data.

1. Who can request deletion?

Any registered Agrica Driver user can request that their account and associated personal data be deleted in line with our Privacy Policy and applicable data-protection laws.

2. How to request deletion

You can request deletion of your account using either method below:

A) In-App Request

  1. Open the Agrica Driver app.
  2. Go to Profile > Help & Support.
  3. Select “Delete my account”.
  4. Submit your request in the form provided.

B) Email Request

Send an email to support@agrica.app from the email address linked to your Agrica Driver account with the subject: “Delete Agrica Driver Account”.

Please include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Phone number used in the app
  • Country/city of registration
  • Confirmation that you want your account permanently deleted

3. What happens after you request deletion?

  • We will verify your identity using the details you provide.
  • Once verified, we will deactivate your account and start the deletion process.
  • In most cases, deletion will be completed within 30 days, unless we are required by law to keep certain records (for example, transaction or anti-fraud logs).
  • After deletion, you will no longer be able to log in or recover your account.

4. Data we may retain

We may keep limited information where we are legally required to do so, or where it is necessary for fraud prevention, audits, tax, or dispute resolution. Any retained data will be stored securely and only for as long as required by law.

If you have any questions about this process, you can contact us at:
