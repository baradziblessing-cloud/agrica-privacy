<h1>Agrica Driver – Privacy Policy</h1>
<p class="meta">Last updated: 30 November 2025</p>

<p>Welcome to Agrica Driver. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Agrica Driver mobile and web applications.</p>

<h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>

<h3>1.1 Information You Provide</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Name, surname, phone number, email address, password.</li>
  <li>Driver verification documents (ID, driver’s licence, vehicle registration, insurance, roadworthy).</li>
  <li>Profile information (photo, vehicle details, drone licence, tractor or truck details).</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.2 Information We Collect Automatically</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Real-time GPS location when you are Online or on a trip/delivery.</li>
  <li>Trip history, delivery history, earnings data.</li>
  <li>Device information, crash reports, diagnostics.</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.3 Financial Information</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Bank account or mobile wallet details for payouts.</li>
  <li>Earnings, bonuses, incentives, fees.</li>
</ul>

<h2>2. How We Use Your Information</h2>
<ul>
  <li>To create and manage your account.</li>
  <li>To match you with riders or delivery requests.</li>
  <li>To track trip progress and display accurate ETAs.</li>
  <li>To calculate fares, incentives, and payouts.</li>
  <li>To improve app performance, detect fraud, and keep the platform safe.</li>
  <li>To comply with legal obligations.</li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Sharing Your Information</h2>
<p>We may share your data with:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Riders or customers (name, photo, vehicle details, live location during an active trip).</li>
  <li>Service providers (hosting, analytics, maps, SMS/Email services).</li>
  <li>Law enforcement if legally required.</li>
</ul>

<p>We never sell your personal information.</p>

<h2>4. Data Retention</h2>
<p>We retain your data for as long as required for legal, accounting, or operational reasons, including trip records and payout history.</p>

<h2>5. Your Rights</h2>
<p>You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your information, depending on your location and applicable laws. Contact us to make a request.</p>

<h2>6. Security</h2>
<p>We use encryption, authentication, access controls, and monitoring to protect your data. However, no platform is 100% secure.</p>

<h2>7. International Transfers</h2>
<p>Your data may be stored or processed in different countries. We implement safeguards where necessary.</p>

<h2>8. Children’s Privacy</h2>
<p>This app is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.</p>

<h2>9. Updates to This Policy</h2>
<p>We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the latest version.</p>
