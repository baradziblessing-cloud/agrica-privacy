Agrica Driver – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 November 2025

Welcome to Agrica Driver. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Agrica Driver mobile and web applications.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, surname, phone number, email address, password.
  • Driver verification documents (ID, driver’s licence, vehicle registration, insurance, roadworthy).
  • Profile information (photo, vehicle details, drone licence, tractor or truck details).

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Real-time GPS location when you are Online or on a trip/delivery.
  • Trip history, delivery history, earnings data.
  • Device information, crash reports, diagnostics.

1.3 Financial Information

  • Bank account or mobile wallet details for payouts.
  • Earnings, bonuses, incentives, fees.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match you with riders or delivery requests.
  • To track trip progress and display accurate ETAs.
  • To calculate fares, incentives, and payouts.
  • To improve app performance, detect fraud, and keep the platform safe.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Riders or customers (name, photo, vehicle details, live location during an active trip).
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, maps, SMS/Email services).
  • Law enforcement if legally required.

We never sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as required for legal, accounting, or operational reasons, including trip records and payout history.

5. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your information, depending on your location and applicable laws. Contact us to make a request.

6. Security

We use encryption, authentication, access controls, and monitoring to protect your data. However, no platform is 100% secure.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be stored or processed in different countries. We implement safeguards where necessary.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the latest version.

10. Contact Us
